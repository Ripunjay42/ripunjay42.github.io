My Projects

REAL TIME FACE MASK DETECTOR

The project is called the Face Mask Detection where it uses facial and object recognition to accurately distinguish those with or without masks. The program will use a device (e.g. webcam, camera etc.) through which it will detect whether the person or the persons are wearing face masks or not. If it detects a person without face mask then it will capture an image and store it into the database. This project is needed to prevent higher risk of spreading the coronavirus and to enforce those who don't follow proper guidelines to wear a mask.
